About Kaushal -2.0

Shri Vaishnav Vidyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya, Indore under the aegis of Shri Vaishnav Shaikshanik awam Parmarthik Nyas, Indore (M.P.) is organizing a National Level Computer Society of India Convention– “KAUSHAL-19” on the theme of “Recent Digital Technology advancement” from 15/03/2019 to 16/03/2019. The objective of this National Convention is to provide a phenomenal opportunity for the students to showcase their technical skills, ideas and/or implementations as a cherishing outcome in the contribution to the Nation’s Dream of IT superpower globally.The convention will consisted of 07 events such as workshops, research articles, project contest, key note addresses, web & mobile design application, programming contest & technical quiz contest. The convention will provide fruitful knowledge dissemination and cultural exchange among students from various parts of the country. Attractive Prizes Worth Rs 1,00,000 (Exclusive Awards, Cash Prizes, Speacial Recognitions, Recognized Certificates and many more).

About SVVV

Shri Vaishnav Vidyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya is a private university established under Madhya Pradesh Niji Vishwavidyalaya ( Sthapana Avam Sanchalan) Adhiniyam in 2015 at Indore MP(India). The University has been established with a vision to be leader in shaping better future for mankind through quality education, training and research. It shall pursue the mission to make a difference in sustaining the growth of global societies by developing socially responsible citizens. Value based education being at the helm, the University shall promote endurance, excellence, fairness, honesty and transparency as its core values.

  • Some of the objectives of the University are as under:
  • To provide teaching and training in higher education and make provision for research as well as advancement and dissemination of knowledge.
  • To ensure world class quality in its offerings and create higher levels of intellectual abilities.
  • To create centres of excellence for research and development for sharing knowledge and its applications.

About INDORE

  • Indore lies on a borderline between a humid subtropical climate and a tropical savanna. Because of its high elevation and inland location even during the hottest months the nights are relatively cool, which is known as Shab-e-Malwa.
  • Culture of Indore is an exclusive mixture of different cultures makes it a best destination across the country, Indore has welcomed people from all castes, creed and color. People from all corners of the country have actually migrated and settled in the heart of Madhya Pradesh for various reasons like livelihood, education and its peaceful culture.
  • Indore has been selected as one of the 100 Indian cities to be developed as smart city under the smart cities Mission. Indore has been elected as cleanest city of India two years in a row as per the Swachh Survekshan 2017 and Swachh Survekshan 2018, respectively
  • Pithampur is a town in the Dhar district of Madhya Pradesh, India. Pithampur is an indusrtial city, and has close proximity to Indore.
  • Indore is also education hub of central India which has both IIT and IIM.
  • Indore has also IT Park/Hub which includes IMPETUS, CSC etc. and Indore also has top leading IT companies like TCS, INFOSYS, DIASPARK.
  • Rajwada is a historical place in Indore city. It was built by the Holkers of the Maratha Empire. Khajrana Ganesh Temple is a pilgrimage center in Indore. Mandu or Mandavgad is an ancient city in the present-day Mandav area of the Dhar district. Mahakaleshwar & Omkareshwar are Hindu temples dedicated to God Shiva. It is one of the 12 revered Jyotirlinga shrines of Shiva. The Lal Bagh Palace was built by Maharaja Shivaji Rao Holkar, beautiful Palace is situated in the outskirts of the Indore city.

About CSI-SVVV

CSI accorded the status of a Students Branch(SB) with CSI Student members since July 2011. CSI SB SVVV in association of SVIIT has come to establish itself as one of the most active CSI Student Branch in Indore city through its consistent effort . CSI SB has a workforce of many experienced students who have been working dedicatedly for the conduction of events including Technical fests, social events, seminars, workshops, FDP's & SDP's and guest lectures. CSI-SB has organized total number of 152 events including training, workshop seminar, SDP, FDP & contests till date. SVVV is running student branch of CSI for since 7 years successfully & awarded by CSI many times at national level including Best accredited student branch.
Formed in 1965, the CSI has been instrumental in guiding the Indian IT industry down the right path since its formative years. Today, the CSI has 72 chapters all over India, 511 student branches, and more than 100000 members.
The mission of the CSI is to facilitate research, knowledge sharing, learning and career enhancement for all categories of IT professionals, while simultaneously inspiring and nurturing new entrants into the industry and helping them to integrate into the IT community. As a professional body, the CSI encourages and supports research and development projects in information and communications technologies. It also has also been partially funding the participation of Indian IT professionals at renowned international IT conferences.

Technical Quiz

Project Contest

In this event UG/PG students have to present their project along with running code.
